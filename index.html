Publications

FedCCML: Personalized Federated Learning with Classifier-Centric Mutual Learning

Haotian Shen, Changli Zhou, and Zhenyu Jiang

Accepted at IEEE SMC 2026

A classifier-centric personalized federated learning method that combines a prototype-conditioned hyperhead with classifier-latent mutual learning. The method is evaluated under label-skew and feature-skew settings across five real-world datasets.
